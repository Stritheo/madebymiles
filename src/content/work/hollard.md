## Context

Hollard Insurance acquired Commonwealth Bank's general insurance portfolio, creating a 15-year strategic alliance to serve one million CBA customers. This was one of the largest insurance transactions in Australia, requiring a complete operational transition while maintaining service continuity and meeting Hollard's target returns and risk appetite.

## What I did

**Alliance establishment.** Established the 15-year strategic alliance with CBA including the strategic plan, governance, funding and a fully resourced program and operating model to deliver the plan.

**Portfolio transformation.** Delivered the portfolio changes required to operate within Hollard's risk appetite and generate target returns from the $1 billion in written premiums. Completed within five months.

**Regulatory closure.** Signed the Executive Attestation to close ASIC's Pricing Promise Review. This was based on a control environment that prevented breaches, resulting in no regulatory action taken by ASIC.

**Transition management.** Managed the end-to-end transition of one million customers from CBA's insurance operation to Hollard's platform, ensuring continuity of service and minimal disruption.

## Results

- 15-year strategic alliance established with CBA
- Portfolio changes delivered within five months to meet target returns and risk appetite
- ASIC Pricing Promise Review closed with no regulatory action
- One million customers transitioned successfully
