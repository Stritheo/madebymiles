## Context

Westpac Banking Corporation had established an alliance with Allianz to offer general insurance products to Westpac's customer base. I led a product and operations team of 50 staff. The alliance needed a clear distribution strategy and operating model to realise its potential. Insurance was being distributed through Westpac's branch network, contact centres and emerging digital channels, but lacked the focus and structure to grow.

## What I did

**Distribution strategy.** Developed and implemented the distribution plan for the Westpac-Allianz alliance. Doubled premiums by transferring in portfolios and integrating insurance products with lending processes. Defined channel strategies, set targets and built the processes and reporting needed to drive consistent execution across a national network.

**Operating model.** Designed the operating model for how Westpac and Allianz would work together on a day-to-day basis. Clarified accountabilities, escalation paths and decision rights to ensure the alliance operated efficiently.

**Customer experience.** Improved NPS from +25 to +44 by focusing on the customer experience across all touchpoints and offshoring non-customer-facing operations to free up capacity for frontline service.

## Results

- Written premiums doubled through portfolio transfers and lending integration
- NPS increased from +25 to +44
- 50-person product and operations team
- Distribution plan and operating model established for the Westpac-Allianz alliance
