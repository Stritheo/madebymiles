## Context

Strata Community Insurance is a specialist strata insurer operating in a market segment defined by complexity, compliance burden and legacy systems. The business had experienced three consecutive years of declining direct channel sales, with digital trading contributing less than 5% of total volume. Operational backlogs were growing, underwriting was manual, and the technology roadmap had stalled.

## What I did

I was brought in to build a data and technology capability from the ground up. The mandate was clear: shift the business from manual operations to digitally enabled, data-driven decision-making.

**Digital channels.** Redesigned the digital trading platform and sales funnel, shifting digital trading from less than 5% to 60% of total volume. Direct channel sales quadrupled within the first year after three years of decline.

**Pricing sophistication.** Introduced granular pricing models that better reflected risk, improving both competitiveness and portfolio quality.

**AI automation.** Deployed agentic AI for underwriting triage with 97% accuracy, enabling the team to focus on complex risks rather than routine processing. Built a governance framework for responsible AI use aligned to emerging regulatory expectations.

**Operational discipline.** Cleared a backlog of 30,000 tasks that had accumulated over years. Reduced underwriting cycle time by 19% through process redesign and automation.

## Results

- Digital trading shifted from less than 5% to 60% of volume
- Direct channel sales quadrupled after three years of decline
- 30,000-task backlog cleared
- Underwriting cycle time reduced by 19%
- AI deployed with 97% accuracy in underwriting triage
- 76% staff engagement score
