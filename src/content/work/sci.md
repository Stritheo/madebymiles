## Context

Strata Community Insurance is a specialist strata insurance managed general agent, 90% intermediated through brokers. The direct business had no meaningful digital presence: an outdated website feeding a manual process across fragmented national branches. Three consecutive years of declining direct channel sales. No workforce planning, demand forecasting, or capacity management. The organisation had no prior experience with data-driven decision-making, agentic AI, or digital-first operating models.

## What I did

**Year 1, H1: Operational foundation.** Established a national operations team. Cleared a backlog of 30,000 tasks in two months with unchanged resourcing. Reduced underwriting cycle time by 19%. Appointed two team leaders: one external hire and one promoted internally. The internal promotion subsequently led the entire national operations team.

**Year 1, H2: First digital iteration.** Launched a new website with digital forms as a test-and-learn. That October, a competitor faced a major industry investigation. Call volumes doubled. Website traffic increased tenfold. Service levels improved because the operational capacity built in H1 absorbed the demand. The volume increase was sustained.

**Year 2, H1: Website rebuild.** Complete rebuild: new visual identity, SEO and LLM optimisation, improved user experience.

**Year 2, H2: End-to-end digital underwriting and AI.** Built a complete self-serve digital acquisition journey: strata plan data pre-population via plan number lookup, adjustable form fields, claims history upload, excess and option selection, real-time pricing, multiple payment methods. Online claims lodgement with API to a major broker eliminating double-keying. Deployed agentic AI for underwriting email and attachment processing at 97% accuracy, direct to policy admin via API. AI tools to 20% of technical users. Broker portal redesigned with RPA and API integrations. AI governance framework built.

## Results

- Digital availability from less than 5% to 60%
- 30,000-task backlog cleared in two months, unchanged resourcing
- Underwriting cycle time reduced 19%
- Agentic AI deployed at 97% accuracy
- Staff engagement 76%
- Absorbed doubled call volumes and 10x web traffic from competitor crisis with improved service levels
- Strong leadership succession in place
